"Pão de Queijo"

Description

Experience the irresistible allure of Pão de Queijo, a beloved Brazilian treat that combines the perfect balance of cheesy goodness and pillowy softness. Made with tapioca flour and cheese, these delectable cheese bread balls are baked to golden perfection, resulting in a crispy exterior and a delightfully chewy interior. Whether enjoyed as a snack, breakfast item, or accompaniment to your favorite dishes, Pão de Queijo is guaranteed to bring a taste of Brazil to your table and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups tapioca flour (polvilho)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups grated cheese (such as Parmesan or mozzarella)
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a mini muffin tin or line with paper liners.
  2. In a saucepan, combine milk, vegetable oil, and salt. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in tapioca flour until well combined and a smooth dough forms.
  4. Let the dough cool slightly, then mix in grated cheese and eggs until evenly distributed.
  5. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of the dough and place them into the prepared muffin tin.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until puffed up and golden brown.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving.
  8. Enjoy warm as a delightful snack or accompaniment to your favorite meals.
