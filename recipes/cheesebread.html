"Pão de Queijo"

Description

Experience the irresistible allure of Pão de Queijo, a beloved Brazilian treat that combines the perfect balance of cheesy goodness and pillowy softness. Made with tapioca flour and cheese, these delectable cheese bread balls are baked to golden perfection, resulting in a crispy exterior and a delightfully chewy interior. Whether enjoyed as a snack, breakfast item, or accompaniment to your favorite dishes, Pão de Queijo is guaranteed to bring a taste of Brazil to your table and leave you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups tapioca flour (polvilho)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups grated cheese (such as Parmesan or mozzarella)
  • 2 eggs
