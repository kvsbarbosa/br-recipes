"Coxinha"

Description

Indulge in the savory delight of Coxinha, a Brazilian favorite. These golden, crispy snacks feature a creamy chicken filling, encased in a flavorful dough, and then deep-fried to perfection. With each bite, experience a burst of rich, comforting flavors that will transport you to the streets of Brazil. Perfect for snacking or as a crowd-pleasing appetizer, Coxinha is sure to satisfy your cravings for something deliciously satisfying.
